How To

Have you ever woken up, opened your refridgerator and found it completely empty? With your stomach rumbling, you flip through your cookbooks trying to find a recipe that you can cook, but you find nothing that you can cook with an an apple, flour, sugar, oil, and salt. With our new application you can easily find recipes that fit best with the ingredients you have at hand. Type out a list of ingedients, and our algorithim will find the best and tastiest match from your list.

About

Hello! we are the developes of CookBook, a webapp designed to return relevant recipes based on ingrediants. The webapp was developed by Jack Liu, Zach Nguyen and Victor Tran, who are all AP Computer Science students in Thomas Jefferson High School. The origin of this program was from the three of us attending our school's renown hackathon, HackTJ, on March 25-26. The idea came from us, young men who wanted to cook a variety of foods but did not know where to start based on our ingredients.
